Director of Risk Reduction Management · May 2022–Oct 2024

  • Led Sendai Framework reporting and the Ministry’s mid-term voluntary report to the UN Office for Disaster Risk Reduction.

Federal Government Transition Team — Mexico City

Advisor of the proposal Subminister of Regional Development on Disaster Risk Reduction Management Policy · Jun 2018–Dec 2018

  • Participated in the design of the public policy for the National Reconstruction Program (PNR) 2019–2024, a priority program of the Federal Government, including the development of a multisectoral diagnostic following the 2017 earthquakes (208,326 damages assessed in housing, infrastructure, and cultural heritage); development of a stratified damage sampling (n=3,250) to define the program’s scope; design of operational guidelines, implementation, monitoring, and supervision mechanisms.
